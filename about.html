Our Story

Founded in 2018, Flora & Co began as a small flower stall in Surabaya with a big dream - to bring beauty and joy through flowers to every special moment in people's lives.

Our Mission

To create meaningful floral arrangements that celebrate life's special moments while maintaining sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To become Indonesia's most trusted floral brand, known for exceptional quality, creativity, and customer service.

Our Values

Passion for flowers, commitment to quality, respect for nature, and dedication to our customers' happiness.

Our Process

Every bouquet tells a story, and we're meticulous about every detail:

  • Sourcing the freshest flowers from local growers
  • Hand-selecting each stem for quality
  • Designing with color theory and composition in mind
  • Carefully packaging for protection and presentation
  • Delivering with care to your doorstep
